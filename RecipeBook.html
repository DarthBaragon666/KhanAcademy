Recipe #1
Emo feast

  • Time: 2 hours
  • Serves: 5 people
Ingredients Quantity
Emo intestines 2 (each intestine)
Emo liver 1
Emo heart 1
Emo lungs 2 (each lung)
Emo muscles As mamy as you can cook

Step 1: Take the dead body of the emo you just killed and start separating the organs.
Step 2: Take the intestines and spread them evenly on a tray. It doesn't matter if we cook our meat because we're death metal as heck so salmonella actually helps us
Step 3: Cut the emo liver into squares and put toothpicks to have a gourmet feel.
Step4: Wash the emo heart to get rid of any blood that may be on it.
Step 5: Cut the emo lungs into short pieces of the alveoli like broccoli.
Step 6: Rip each emo muscle apart from each other and make sure you get the good ones like the calves, biceps, glutes, etc.
Congratulations! Now you're ready to have an emo feast with your cannibal family!

Recipe #2
Bloody Baconator

  • Time: 5 minutes
  • Serves: 1 person
Ingredients Quantity
Raw ground beef patty 2
Raw bacon strip 6
Blood 1 small cup
Pus 1 small cup
Buns 2
Cheese 2 singles

Step 1: Put your first cheese single on the bottom bun.
Step 2: Put the first patty on top of the cheese.
Step 3: Put 3 bacon strips on top of the first patty.
Step 4: Put the second cheese single on top of the bacon strips.
Step 5: Put the second patty on top of the second cheese single.
Step 6: Put 3 bacon strips on top of the second patty.
Step 7: Put the blood and pus on top of the bacon strips.
Step 8: Put the top bun on top.
Congratulations! You have made a meal fit for a king or queen like yourself.
