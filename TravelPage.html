Travel to Toronto

Here are some sites you can go.

Ripley's Aquarium

Things in Ripley's Aquarium

  • Wildlife from waters all around the world
  • Petting horseshoe crabs
  • A glass tunnel surrounded by water and fish

Canada's Wonderland

Canada's Wonderland is an amusement park in a town near Toronto called Vaughan. It's super fun with a roller coaster that goes as high as 306 feet, lots of loops, a fun waterpark, and good restaurants.

The Toronto Zoo

The Toronto Zoo is a fun place with animals from almost every continent. It has tours around the place in the Zoomobile, the zookeepers come to the enclosure to talk about the animals to the patrons, there are multiple restaurants for any taste.

The CN Tower

The CN Tower has cool things like...

  • A glass floor
  • A balcony to view outdoors
  • A very good restaurant
